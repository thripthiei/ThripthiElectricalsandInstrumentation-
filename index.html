About

We are an Authorised Service Center of Crompton Greaves Consumer Electricals Limited & CG Power and Industrial Solutions Limited. Based in Kochi, We have a legacy of more than 40 years in the field of Pump and Motor Servicing. Our Services are maintaining and repairing the Pumps of Crompton Greaves Consumer Electricals Limited and the Motors of CG Power and Industrial Solutions Limited during and after the product Warranty. We maintain a collection of Spares for the Pumps of Crompton Greaves Consumer Electricals Limited and for the Motors of CG Power and Industrial Solutions Limited.
